Background

Greetings! My name is Tim Milker, and I'm delighted to offer you a glimpse into my background and journey thus far.

I was born and raised in the vibrant city of Bonn, where I spent my formative years soaking in the rich culture and diverse opportunities it had to offer. After completing my secondary education, I had the privilege of attending the esteemed Bertolt Brecht Gesamtschule in Tannenbusch, where I obtained my Abitur. This period of my life was marked by academic rigor, personal growth, and the forging of lifelong friendships.

At 24 years old, I find myself on an exciting path of professional development. Currently, I am engaged in an apprenticeship at Deutsche Telekom AG GmbH, where I am training to become a Fachinformatiker. This role allows me to combine my passion for technology with a hands-on approach to problem-solving and innovation. Each day presents new challenges and opportunities for learning, and I am grateful for the chance to contribute to such a dynamic and forward-thinking company.

Beyond my professional endeavors, I remain deeply connected to my roots in Bonn. The city's rich history and vibrant community continue to inspire me, and I cherish the memories of growing up in such a lively and welcoming environment. Whether it's exploring the picturesque streets of the Altstadt or enjoying the scenic beauty of the Rhine River, Bonn will always hold a special place in my heart.

Looking ahead, I am eager to continue honing my skills, expanding my knowledge, and embracing new experiences. Whether it's through further education, career advancement, or personal growth initiatives, I am committed to pursuing excellence and making a positive impact in everything I do.

In summary, my background is a blend of academic achievement, professional growth, and a deep-rooted connection to my hometown of Bonn. Each chapter of my journey has shaped me into the person I am today, and I am excited to see where the road ahead will lead.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my story!
